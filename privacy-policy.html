Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the fan-developed browser page for Home Is Where He Is (the "Site"), hosted at https://homeiswhereheis.org and its subpages. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how this Site operates.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data. This Site does not use cookies, tracking scripts, analytics services, or any form of user data collection. We do not create user accounts, maintain databases, or log visitor activity.

2. Automatic Data (Server Logs)

When you visit the Site, your browser automatically sends certain information that may be logged by GitHub Pages (the hosting provider). This may include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and the date/time of your request. This data is handled by GitHub's infrastructure and is subject to GitHub's Privacy Statement. We do not access or use this data.

3. Third-Party Services

This Site embeds or links to third-party services that have their own privacy practices:

  • Google Fonts: The Site loads font files from Google Fonts API. When you load a page, your browser requests the fonts from Google, which may log your IP address. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube (nocookie): Embedded videos use the youtube-nocookie.com domain, which limits Google's ability to track you via embedded videos. Some basic data (e.g., video playback) may still be collected by YouTube/Google. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Game Embed (playminigames.net): The playable game is embedded via an iframe from playminigames.net. That site may set its own cookies or collect data. We have no control over their practices. Review their policy if you have concerns.
  • Itch.io Link: The download link directs to Itch.io (echobat's game page). Itch.io has its own Privacy Policy.

4. Game Save Data

The embedded Ren'Py game may use your browser's local storage (IndexedDB / localStorage) to save game progress. This data is stored entirely on your device. It is never transmitted to our server or any third party. Clearing your browser storage will delete this data.

5. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at children under 13. The game itself is rated for mature audiences (18+). We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal data. Since we do not collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete on our end. If you are concerned about data collected by third-party services (Google Fonts, YouTube, etc.), please review their respective privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Contact

This is a fan-developed page. For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the game developer echobat via their Itch.io page.
